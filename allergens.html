Allergen Information

What's In It?

We produce our food in kitchens where allergens are handled by the team, but where equipment and utensils are used for multiple menu items, including those containing allergens.

While we try to keep things separate, we can't guarantee any item is allergen free even after ingredients have been removed on request.

⚠️ Please inform our staff of any allergies before ordering. Your safety is our priority.
